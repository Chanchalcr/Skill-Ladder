About Us

Skill ladder is an educational platform that focuses on developing fundamental and crucial skills in students that are instrumental in modern technologies. Skill ladder provides affordable courses in multiple fields that students from different parts of the country will find very helpful in their future.

Our Mission

We believe in Atmanirbhar Bharat. We believe that real education can only be truly imparted through real life implementations far more effectivey than standardized tests based on mugging up theories. To that end, Skill ladder provides practical skills and knowledge to its users through carefully curated and aplication based courses and projects taught by some of the finest instructors in the country. Skill ladder comes in different Reigonal languages so that it can be used in different parts of the country and all students can use it to their advantage.

Why us and Our end goal

Statistics from the AISHE 2019-20 by MHRD show that of the 30 Lakh+ students graduate with 6 Lakh engineers, and 80% of those engineers do not possess the skills required in the current industry, and hence they eventually struggle in the industry. Here at Skill Ladder, we strive to bring a change with completely indigenous courses taught by Indian instructors. We want to make the youth of India more employable and self-dependent.
